About Us

Grace & Gloss — The Gloss That Loves Your Lips

Grace & Gloss is a lip gloss brand created to reign for a lifetime — not just for a moment. Founded by Adeola and inspired by faith, passion, and purpose, the brand was thoughtfully prayed into existence and brought to life with love.

Our gloss is one of a kind. It shines effortlessly, glows naturally, and feels light with no pressure on the lips. Each formula is carefully crafted with a unique texture, scent, and finish, while every gloss is beautifully packaged with its own distinct aesthetic.

At Grace & Gloss, we go beyond beauty. We prioritize lip health by including a lip care experience within our brand — because we don’t just gloss, we care. This is what truly sets us apart.
